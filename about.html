About NIVPL

What began as a humble restaurant in Belagavi has today evolved into one of the region’s most respected names in hospitality. N I Ventures Private Limited (NIVPL) stands as the corporate home to the Niyaaz Group of Restaurants — a collection of diverse brands built on the shared foundation of taste, trust, and timeless quality.

From that first kitchen on P.B. Road, NIVPL has grown into a multi-brand enterprise that spans dine-in, delivery, and takeaway concepts. Yet, the essence remains unchanged — creating food experiences that bring people together. Every brand under NIVPL carries forward the same passion for authenticity, consistency, and warmth that started it all.

Driven by vision, rooted in values, and powered by people, NIVPL continues to shape the way Belagavi and beyond experience good food.

Vision

To be the region’s most trusted and admired restaurant group — one that consistently delivers excellence through flavour, service, and integrity.

We envision NIVPL as a brand that not only leads in business but also inspires confidence in every guest, partner, and team member who becomes part of its journey.

Our vision goes beyond growth; it is about creating experiences that people remember, nurturing a sense of belonging through every meal, and setting new benchmarks for hospitality across the region. By blending tradition with innovation and expertise with empathy, we aim to build a name synonymous with reliability, value, and heartfelt service.

Mission

Our mission is to build and nurture food brands that bring people together through authentic flavours, consistent quality, and genuine hospitality.

  • Maintaining uncompromising standards of taste, hygiene, and service across all our brands.
  • Empowering our teams through expert training, strong systems, and a culture of pride in craftsmanship.
  • Offering exceptional value at competitive prices, without ever compromising on quality.
  • BBuilding long-term partnerships with suppliers, stakeholders, and communities based on mutual respect and trust.
  • Continuously innovating to meet the evolving needs of our guests while staying true to our roots.

Our Story

The story of NIVPL is deeply rooted in the vision of Mr. Soudagar and his sons, Mr. Niyaz Soudagar and Mr. Irshad Soudagar — a family driven by the belief that great food has the power to bring people together.

What began as a simple idea in the heart of Belagavi’s old city soon grew into a defining movement in the region’s dining culture. At a time when eating out with family and friends was not yet a norm, the Soudagars envisioned a space where people could share meals, celebrate moments, and experience hospitality that felt personal.

From that vision emerged a series of restaurants and concepts that combined dining, takeaway, and home delivery, built around one unwavering principle the customer comes first. Every decision, every recipe, and every outlet was created with quality, consistency, and trust at its core.

Our Purpose

At NIVPL, our purpose has always been to make dining an experience worth sharing. We wanted to redefine how Belagavi came together over food to create spaces where families gathered, friends reconnected, and celebrations found their flavour.

By nurturing a culture of hospitality that values people as much as food, we’ve built more than just restaurants we’ve built relationships that last.

What Makes Us Different

What truly sets NIVPL apart is not just what we serve, but how we serve.

Our foundation rests on trust, quality, and an enduring commitment to excellence. We don’t see service as a transaction, but as a promise to deliver warmth, consistency, and care, every single time.

It’s this spirit of perseverance and passion that has turned a single family vision into a trusted name, and a beloved experience for thousands of guests across generations.
