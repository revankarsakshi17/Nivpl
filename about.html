Who We Are

What began as a humble restaurant in Belagavi has today evolved into one of the region's most respected names in hospitality.

N I Ventures Private Limited (NIVPL) stands as the corporate home to the Niyaaz Group of Restaurants — a collection of diverse brands built on the shared foundation of taste, trust, and timeless quality.

From that first kitchen on P.B. Road, NIVPL has grown into a multi-brand enterprise that spans dine-in, delivery, and takeaway concepts. Yet, the essence remains unchanged — creating food experiences that bring people together.

Every brand under NIVPL carries forward the same passion for authenticity, consistency, and warmth that started it all. Driven by vision, rooted in values, and powered by people, NIVPL continues to shape the way Belagavi and beyond experiences good food.

Our Journey

Three and a Half Decades
of Flavour

From a single kitchen to a multi-brand legacy

1989
The Beginning

On 20th October, the first Niyaaz Restaurant opened its doors on P.B. Road, Belagavi — a humble kitchen with an extraordinary dream.

2000s
Brand Expansion

NIVPL began growing beyond its flagship outlet, launching new concepts across dine-in, delivery, and takeaway formats.

2010s
Corporate Identity

N I Ventures Private Limited was formally established, giving the group a unified corporate identity to house its growing portfolio of brands.

Today
A Thriving Legacy

NIVPL stands as a multi-brand hospitality enterprise — trusted by thousands of guests, celebrated across the region, and still driven by day one passion.
