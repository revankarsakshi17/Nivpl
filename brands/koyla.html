Koyla by Niyaaz

Koyla is our tribute to the timeless art of cooking over fire, a celebration of smoky aromas, slow grilling and years of tradition that awaken every sense. Inspired by traditional tandoor and charcoal-style techniques, Koyla brings the essence of rustic Indian cooking into a modern dining experience.

Here, every dish is crafted with care and patience, allowing the flames to infuse their magic into every bite. From tender kebabs and rich gravies to freshly baked breads, each plate carries that unmistakable depth of flavour that only live-fire cooking can create.

Koyla isn’t just about food it’s about an experience. The glow of the charcoal, the aroma of marinated spices, and the warmth of a hearty meal come together to create something unforgettable. It’s indulgence that feels earned, flavour that feels alive, and dining that feels truly satisfying.
