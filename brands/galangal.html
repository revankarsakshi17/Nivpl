Galangal by Niyaaz

Galangal brings the essence of Pan-Asian flavours to you through a place where every dish captures the soul of Asian cuisine. Inspired by the culinary traditions of Thailand, Korea, Japan, and China, it blends authentic recipes with a modern touch to create your food.

Each dish is thoughtfully prepared to achieve the perfect balance of taste and texture. From wok-tossed noodles and hearty curries to signature sauces and rice bowls, every bite offers a mix of comfort and excitement.

Created with a focus on quality and detail, Galangal introduces you to the richness of Asian dining food that feels approachable yet distinctive. It’s a space where flavours come alive, stories are shared through every ingredient, and dining becomes an experience worth remembering.
