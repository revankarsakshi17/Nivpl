Assal Mawla Darbar by Niyaaz

Assal Mawla Darbar is our homage to the royal kitchens of Kolhapur, a brand that celebrates the city’s rich culinary legacy through dishes deeply rooted in tradition.

Created to honour the grandeur of Kolhapuri cuisine, Assal Mawla Darbar captures the spirit of royal feasts while staying true to authentic regional recipes. Each dish tells a story of spice, heritage, and the pride of a culture that values hospitality as much as flavour.

From aromatic gravies to slow-cooked delicacies, every meal at the Darbar is designed to feel grand yet comforting, indulgent yet familiar. It’s where tradition meets emotion, and every guest is treated not just as a customer, but as a part of the family.

Assal Mawla Darbar is more than a restaurant it’s a heartfelt tribute to Kolhapur’s timeless food heritage.
