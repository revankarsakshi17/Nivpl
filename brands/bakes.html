Bakes by Niyaaz

Bakes by Niyaaz is where sweetness meets soul our baked-specialty extension created for moments of joy and comfort. Carrying forward the trusted Niyaaz legacy, this concept brings oven-fresh goodness to every occasion, big or small.

From soft breads and savoury bites to decadent cakes and desserts, Bakes by Niyaaz celebrates the art of baking with the same dedication to quality and care that defines every Niyaaz brand. Each item is crafted to balance flavour, freshness, and finesse ensuring that every bite feels like a warm embrace.

Whether it’s a festive celebration, a quick coffee break, or an everyday craving, Bakes by Niyaaz offers something to make the moment special. Because here, baking isn’t just about recipes it’s about spreading happiness, one perfectly baked treat at a time.
