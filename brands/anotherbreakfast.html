Another Breakfast by Niyaaz

Another Breakfast was born from a simple idea to make mornings exciting again. As Belagavi’s dedicated breakfast concept, it brings together the heart of Indian mornings and the charm of continental classics in one warm, welcoming space.

From buttery parathas and fluffy omelettes to sausages, pancakes, and slow-brewed coffee, every dish here is made to start your day right. Whether you’re rushing for work or meeting friends on a lazy Sunday, Another Breakfast offers something that feels just right familiar, fulfilling, and full of flavour.

More than just a restaurant, it’s a morning ritual. With thoughtful menus, fresh ingredients, and a touch of indulgence, Another Breakfast redefines how Belagavi enjoys its first meal of the day not just as food, but as a moment to pause, savour, and smile.
