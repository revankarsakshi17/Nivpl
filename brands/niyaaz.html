Niyaaz

Niyaaz is the heart of our culinary story—the flagship brand that gave shape and soul to everything NIVPL stands for today. What began as a humble restaurant in Belagavi has grown into a name synonymous with authenticity, trust, and timeless flavour.

Born from a simple passion for good food, Niyaaz set out to serve dishes that carried the warmth of home and the richness of tradition. Every recipe was crafted with care, every meal prepared with honesty—creating flavours that became part of Belagavi’s food culture.

From our signature biryanis to a wide range of Indian and regional favourites, Niyaaz stands for consistency, quality, and taste that never fades. It’s not just a place to eat—it’s where families celebrate, friends connect, and memories are made over food that feels familiar yet special every single time.

Through years of dedication, Niyaaz has grown beyond being a restaurant—it has become a tradition cherished by generations who trust us for the experience they first fell in love with.

At Niyaaz, every plate tells a story of passion, perseverance, and the joy of serving food that brings people together.
